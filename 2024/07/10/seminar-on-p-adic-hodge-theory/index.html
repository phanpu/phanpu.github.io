A reading seminar on $p$-adic Hodge theory

f.p.
2024-07-10
seminars
p-adic Hodge theory, seminars

This is the page of a reading seminar for graduate or undergraduate students organized by me, aiming to thoroughly understand some basic results in $p$-adic Hodge theory. The textbook is Brian Conrad’s book.

Main References

  • Brian Conrad, CMI SUMMER SCHOOL NOTES ON $p$-ADIC HODGE THEORY
  • Ouyang Yi and Jean-Marc Fontaine, Theory of $p$-adic Galois Representations
  • Serin Hong, Notes on $p$-adic Hodge theory

Location and Time

Thursday and Sunday 8:00pm, online.
